Quick Tips:
Preferably use a clear, non-distorted spectral image (800px wide) without grid lines or peak labels.
After processing, preview the extracted curve, verify major peaks with the mouse, then download the CSV file.
Three extraction methods are available: automatic, color-based, and manual point-by-point tracing.

Crop & Set Parameters

Step 2 Tips
1) Adjust the crop box so its top touches the curve's peak and bottom touches the curve's trough.
2) Align left/right edges with spectral start/end points or axis ticks, then enter the wavenumbers accurately.
3) If the axis is non-linear, check "Use 2000 cm⁻¹ split" and drag the yellow dashed line to align with the 2000 wavenumber tick.
